PixelJoy Draw

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

PixelJoy Draw is a children's creativity app focused on drawing, coloring, tracing, pixel art, and guided lessons. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information Stored in the App

PixelJoy Draw stores artwork, child profile names, selected avatars, progress, rewards, and purchase unlock status locally on the device so the app can work properly for the family using it.

Photos

If a parent chooses to use the photo trace feature, the app asks for access to the photo library so a photo can be imported into the app as a trace guide. Imported trace photos are stored locally on the device unless a parent deletes them by deleting the related artwork or deleting the app.

Exports

If a parent chooses to export artwork, the app may save an image to the device photo library or open the system share sheet to let the parent share the export. Export actions are initiated by the user.

In-App Purchases

PixelJoy Draw offers optional non-consumable in-app purchases for extra content. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. The app stores local unlock status on the device and may check purchase entitlements through Apple when needed, such as purchase restore.

Advertising

PixelJoy Draw uses Google AdMob to show banner ads. Ad requests are configured as child-directed, non-personalized, and limited to Google's General audience ad content rating. The app does not request App Tracking Transparency permission and does not use the advertising identifier for personalized advertising.

Google may process limited device and ad request information to deliver, measure, and protect ads. Learn more about Google's ad practices at policies.google.com/technologies/ads and AdMob policies at support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543.

Data Collection and Tracking

PixelJoy Draw does not include third-party analytics, public posting, chat, comments, or social networking features in this version. The app is designed not to track users across apps or websites.

Children's Privacy

The app is designed for kids and families. Parent-sensitive areas such as purchases, exports, and photo import are protected with a parent check inside the app.

Data Sharing

The app does not sell user data. Artwork and profile information stay on the device unless a parent chooses to export artwork using system features. Limited ad request information is shared with Google AdMob for advertising delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and policy compliance.

Data Deletion

Families can delete saved artwork or child profiles from within the app. Deleting the app from the device also removes locally stored app data from that device.
